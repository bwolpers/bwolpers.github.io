5. Star Wars: Episode VII - The Force Awakens

With a lifetime gross of $2,071,310,218, The Force Awakens just barely makes the top 5. Avengers: Infinity War falls just short at the 6th spot.

4. Titanic

With a lifetime gross of $2,264,743,305, Titanic is ahead of The Force Awakens by a good margin.

3. Avatar: The Way of Water

With a lifetime gross of $2,320,250,281, Avatar: The Way of Water makes an impressive leap to the 3rd spot while only having been out for 1 year.

2. Avengers: Endgame

With a lifetime gross of $2,799,439,100, Avengers: Endgame takes the 2nd spot by a huge margin.

1. Avatar

Finally, with an impressive lifetime gross of $2,923,706,026, Avatar takes the top spot without much competition.
